Experimental Conclusion

  1. the model with balanced layers (encode layers=decode layers) performs the best.
  2. if the roles that in datasets cannot be naturally distinguished, adding role information will hurts the model performance.
  3. pre-train from scratch better than further pre-trained from CPM, a long-document pre-trained generative model except on Knowledge Q&As.
  4. when combined with sampling, repetition control, and length penalty, beam search has relatively balanced performance.

Weaknesses

Consistency:

intra-contradiction: contradictions within the response.

inter-contradiction: contradictions between the dialogue context and the response.

Solution: train a contradiction detection classifier to re-rank the generated responses.

Problem: a lack of NLI datasets in Chinese.

Knowledge:

open-domain dialogues are often knowledge-sparse. So the knowledge skills of the pre-trained dialogue models are relatively limited.

Solution:

Implicit: fine-tuning the pre-trained model on crowd-sourced knowledge-intensive dialogue datasets.

Explicit: incorporating information retrieval into generation systems.

Problem: require extensive human-annotated knowledge-grounded dialogue data.

Safety:

unsafe behaviors such as suicide risk ignorance and social bias.

So

post-processing module of dialogue systems;

some methods based on safety detectors;

train models on extra safety-augmentation samples.

Empathy:

Solution: incorporating empathy-demanded semantic information
